A Simple Countdown Timer

Welcome!

This is a simple countdown timer that can count down from 1 to 60 seconds.

Feel free to try it out by entering a number in the box below, or clicking/using the arrows to select a number, then press the
[Start Countdown]
button to initiate the countdown.

New Feature! => You may now initiate multiple countdowns!
